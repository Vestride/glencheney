“Split The Bill”

Allows a group of people to split up a bill, including tax and tip, based on what they bought.

Created by Allison Carnwath, Glen Cheney, and Mike Higham.

Split the Bill Features
  • Input names of people in party
  • Input items ordered and price for each person
  • Allow multiple people to split an item
  • Choose how much to tip
  • Select which state you're in or input tax rate
  • Display which people owe what
User Stories

As a restaurant customer, I want the bill split based on what I bought so that I am not paying for my meal and my friend's expensive meal.

App startup: The user's current bill will be shown.

App closure: The current bill is saved so that the user can come back to it later.

Competitive Analysis
Tip and Split (http://goo.gl/lm6V6)
  • occasionally crashes
  • splits the bill evenly
Check Please (lite) (http://goo.gl/pjyNQ)
  • Saves last entry
  • Features: round tip, round total, post to “PocketMonkey,” change currency, change the default tax rate
  • Splits the tip and bill evenly among selected number of people and tip %
Share-a-bill (lite) (http://goo.gl/umGZV)
  • Allows people to split a bill under a selected ratio
  • Transfer money between people
  • Not quite sure what the purpose is (confusing language/interface)
  • No tax or tip calculations
Billsplit
  • One screen
  • Input total, # in party, amount of tip, amount of tax (pre or post total?), round up or down to nearest 1.00, .50, .25 or .10.
  • Splits bill evenly amongst the number in party
